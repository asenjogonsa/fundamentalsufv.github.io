Fundamentals of Automotive Engineering

Introduction

Automotive engineering is a broad field that encompasses the design, development, and manufacturing of cars. It is a dynamic and challenging field that requires a deep understanding of physics, mathematics, and engineering principles.

Mechanics of Vehicles

Mechanics of vehicles is a fundamental area of automotive engineering that deals with the motion of cars, the forces acting on them, and the principles of energy and momentum transfer. It is essential for understanding how cars accelerate, decelerate, and handle.

Materials Science for Automotive Applications

Materials science is another important area of automotive engineering that deals with the selection and use of materials for car components. It is essential for ensuring that cars are strong, lightweight, and durable.

Electrical Systems in Automotive Vehicles

Electrical systems are becoming increasingly important in cars, with the introduction of hybrid and electric vehicles. Automotive engineers need to understand the principles of electrical engineering to design and develop efficient and reliable electrical systems.
